# Doctor Briefing Report — Template & Generation Prompt

## Report Structure

The doctor's briefing is a Japanese-language document structured in four sections, designed to be immediately useful within a physician's existing workflow.

---

### Section 1: 診察内容の要約 (Visit Summary)

A concise narrative of what was discussed. Two to three paragraphs. Factual, clinical tone. This mirrors what a doctor would write in their own SOAP notes but captures the full conversation content rather than what they had time to document.

### Section 2: 主な医療情報 (Key Medical Information)

Structured extraction of medical entities:

- **症状・主訴** — Symptoms and chief complaints discussed
- **診断・評価** — Diagnosis or assessment mentioned
- **処方・治療** — Medications prescribed or changed, treatments recommended
- **検査** — Tests ordered or results discussed
- **指示事項** — Instructions given to patient (lifestyle, diet, activity, follow-up scheduling)

### Section 3: 次回診察に向けて (For the Next Visit)

Observations from the conversation that may help the doctor prepare for the next appointment:

- Topics the patient returned to or seemed uncertain about
- Questions that appeared unasked (hedging language, trailing off, returning to a topic)
- Areas where additional explanation may benefit the patient
- Follow-up items mentioned but not fully addressed

**Critical framing rule:** All items in this section must be framed as *observations from the conversation*, not clinical assessments. The AI observes behavior; the doctor interprets significance.

- ✅ "患者様は副作用について3回質問されました" (The patient asked about side effects three times)
- ✅ "薬の服用時間について確認が必要かもしれません" (The patient may need clarification on medication timing)
- ❌ "患者様は健康不安を抱えています" (The patient has health anxiety) — this is clinical interpretation, not permitted in v1

### Section 4: 患者からのメモ (Patient Notes)

Left blank in the generated report. The patient fills this in during their review step with their own comments, forgotten items, or questions for the next visit.

### Footer

```
本報告書はAIにより診察の録音から自動生成されたものです。
コミュニケーション支援を目的としており、医療記録としての使用を意図していません。
内容については担当医師による確認をお願いいたします。

Kenkoumon — AI診察コミュニケーション支援ツール
生成日: [DATE]
```

Translation: "This report was automatically generated by AI from a recording of the consultation. It is intended to assist communication and is not intended for use as a medical record. Please verify all content with the treating physician."

## Generation Prompt

Copy the prompt below and paste the transcript where indicated. Use with Claude, GPT-4, or any capable LLM.

````
You are a medical communication assistant. You have received a transcript
of a doctor-patient consultation conducted in Japanese.

Your task: Generate a structured report IN JAPANESE intended for the
treating physician to review before the patient's next visit.

## Rules

1. Be clinically accurate to what was discussed. Do not infer medical
   information beyond what is explicitly stated in the conversation.
2. Write in appropriate medical Japanese. Use 敬語 for the document
   structure and clinical terminology as used in the conversation.
3. For Section 3 (次回診察に向けて), frame ALL items as observations
   from the conversation, not clinical assessments. Describe what the
   patient said or how they behaved, not what you think it means
   clinically.
4. If something is unclear in the transcript, note it as unclear rather
   than guessing.
5. Preserve medication names exactly as spoken. Include both Japanese
   and international names if both were used.

## Output Structure

Generate the report with these exact section headers:

### 診察内容の要約
A concise narrative summary of the consultation in 2-3 paragraphs.
Cover: reason for visit, key discussion points, decisions made,
and next steps agreed upon.

### 主な医療情報
Extract into these sub-categories (omit any category with no relevant
information):

**症状・主訴:**
- [list each symptom or complaint discussed]

**診断・評価:**
- [list any diagnosis or clinical assessment mentioned]

**処方・治療:**
- [list medications prescribed/changed with dosage if mentioned]
- [list any treatments recommended]

**検査:**
- [list tests ordered or results discussed]

**指示事項:**
- [list all instructions given to the patient]
- [include follow-up scheduling if mentioned]

### 次回診察に向けて
Based ONLY on observable patterns in the conversation, note:
- Topics the patient returned to multiple times
- Questions the patient started but did not complete
- Areas where the patient appeared to need more explanation
- Subjects where the patient's responses were notably brief or hesitant
- Any follow-up items mentioned but not fully resolved

Frame each observation factually. Example:
✓ "患者様は薬の副作用について3度言及されました"
✗ "患者様は副作用について不安を感じています"

If no notable observations exist, state: "特記すべき事項はありません。"

### 患者からのメモ
（患者記入欄）

---
本報告書はAIにより診察の録音から自動生成されたものです。
コミュニケーション支援を目的としており、医療記録としての使用を意図していません。
内容については担当医師による確認をお願いいたします。

Kenkoumon — AI診察コミュニケーション支援ツール
生成日: [INSERT DATE]

## Transcript

[PASTE JAPANESE TRANSCRIPT HERE]
````
